## Excel Import for Data Migration

When migrating from your old system:

1. Export patient data to Excel with columns:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - DateOfBirth
   - Gender
   - Address
   - MedicalHistory (optional)
   - Allergies (optional)

2. Go to Patients page in FOD Clinic system
3. Click "Import Excel" button
4. Select your Excel file
5. Patients will be imported automatically
